Making the Most of Student Discounts in the UK

By Dev Neupane | [27/10/2024]

Being a student in the UK comes with several perks, and one of the most significant is access to a variety of discounts on everything from travel and food to clothing and tech. Stretching your budget can help you get the most out of your experience without feeling constantly stretched financially. Here’s a guide to maximizing these benefits and saving as much as possible as a student in the UK.

1. Understanding Your Discount Platforms

Many companies and institutions offer exclusive discounts to students through dedicated platforms. Here are a few worth checking out:

  • TOTUM Card: Endorsed by the National Union of Students (NUS), this paid membership offers discounts on hundreds of brands, including tech, travel, and groceries.
  • UNiDAYS: A popular platform offering free access to discounts across a wide range of sectors, from fashion and technology to fitness and entertainment.
  • Student Beans: Another free platform similar to UNiDAYS, with discounts on retail, dining, and travel.

2. Tech Deals: Laptops, Software, and Gadgets

For tech-savvy students, the UK offers great deals on laptops, software subscriptions, and gadgets that make both studying and socializing easier.

  • Apple Education Store: Provides discounts on Apple products with additional perks like free AirPods during back-to-school promotions.
  • Microsoft Office for Students: Many universities offer free or discounted Office 365 subscriptions.
  • Adobe Creative Cloud: Offers up to 60% off its Creative Cloud subscription for students in creative fields.

3. Clothing and Fashion

Updating your wardrobe doesn’t have to be costly with the various discounts available at popular clothing retailers.

  • ASOS: Provides a 10% student discount year-round for those registered on platforms like UNiDAYS.
  • H&M, Urban Outfitters: Offer student discounts through Student Beans and UNiDAYS, typically 10-20% off.

4. Travel Discounts

Exploring the UK and Europe while you’re studying abroad is one of the best parts of the experience. Here are some discounts that make travel more affordable:

  • 16-25 Railcard: Offers a third off train fares across the UK.
  • National Express and Megabus: Both offer student discounts for travel across the UK.
  • ISIC: Provides discounts on international flights, accommodations, and transportation.

5. Food and Dining Out

Dining out doesn’t have to be expensive with student discounts at various chains and restaurants.

  • Gourmet Society and Tastecard: Offer deals like 2-for-1 and 50% off at popular restaurants.
  • Greggs, Pret a Manger: Popular chains occasionally offer student discounts; check on Student Beans for details.

6. Entertainment and Leisure

Enjoying social life and entertainment options in the UK is affordable with the discounts available for students.

  • Spotify and Apple Music: Offer student subscription plans with discounts.
  • Museums and Cinemas: Many museums and cinemas offer discounts through student platforms.

7. Tips for Maximizing Savings

Here are a few tips for maximizing your student discounts:

  • Stack Discounts: Use student discounts during seasonal sales for extra savings.
  • Flash Sales: Watch for flash sales on platforms like UNiDAYS for additional discounts.
  • Verify Early: Register with platforms like UNiDAYS in advance to easily access discounts.

In conclusion, the UK’s student discounts can make a significant difference to your budget, allowing you to make the most of your time as an international student. By using these resources, you’ll be able to get everything from tech essentials and travel options to meals out at discounted prices, giving you more freedom to explore, experience, and enjoy your time in the UK.
