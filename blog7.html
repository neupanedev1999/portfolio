An Exciting Milestone for Nepali Cinema: AnauTho Tirsana Premieres on Amazon Prime

Kathmandu, October 31

Nepali cinema has reached a new milestone as the film AnauTho Tirsana is set to debut on Amazon Prime, marking it as the first Nepali movie to be showcased on this popular OTT platform. With this release, the Nepali film industry is taking a step forward in gaining global recognition, connecting with audiences across the United States, United Kingdom, and Germany.

The film features notable performances by actors such as Bijay Baral, Bikash Lamichhane, and Srishti Khadka. Produced under the joint banner of Stormcrow Production and Stormcrow Framework, AnauTho Tirsana tells a captivating story penned by Baburam Neupane. Audiences can look forward to a unique storyline and quality production, which promises to bring a fresh, Nepali perspective to the Amazon Prime platform.

In addition to its OTT release, AnauTho Tirsana will also grace theaters across Nepal, allowing local viewers to experience the cinematic achievement on the big screen. This historic OTT debut is set to open new doors for Nepali cinema, giving it a wider platform and connecting with the global Nepali diaspora. The international audience is now just a click away from experiencing Nepali storytelling, talent, and culture in an entirely new way.
